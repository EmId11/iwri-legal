1. The app

IWRI is a Forge app for Atlassian Jira Cloud that computes a workflow risk screening metric ("the Score") and associated category flags from sprint and issue data already present in your Jira instance. The app is intended to support team leaders and engineering managers in identifying workflow patterns that may warrant further investigation.

The Score is a screening indicator, not a diagnostic or performance management tool. It should be interpreted alongside qualitative team context. See Section 7 (Disclaimers) below.
